Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of local developers and has grown into one of the most influential technology conferences globally. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of attendees and showcased groundbreaking technologies.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to connect innovators, empower learners, and drive progress in the tech industry through open collaboration and sharing of knowledge.
